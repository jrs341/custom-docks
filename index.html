Calhoun's Custom Docks

Calhoun will work with you to develop a CAD drawing that will depict every detail of your finished dock to ensure you are 100% satisfied before any construction ever begins. From initial design, engineered drawings, permits and final inspection, Calhoun will be there every step of the way. Calhoun is so confident in the quality and craftsmanship of his docks that there is a five year warranty on all new construction floating docks.

Calhoun will also work with you to outfit your dock with; boat lifts, jet ski lifts, dock roofs, sundecks, sitting areas and also decking for your on land needs.
