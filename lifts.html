While a boatlift may seem like a simple system of cables and pulleys, it’s actually a complex balance of forces. Where and how those forces are applied to each of the components in the system will determine the amount of maintenance required and the lifespan of the system. Regularly inspecting your boatlift and keeping your lift well maintained will keep your lift operating trouble free for many years. If you notice similar issues with your boatlift please give us a call for a free estimate.
